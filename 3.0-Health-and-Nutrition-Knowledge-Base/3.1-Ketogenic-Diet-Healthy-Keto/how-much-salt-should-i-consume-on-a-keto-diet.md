# How much salt should I consume on a Keto diet?

Always use sea salt. If you are on a high-carb diet, you will retain sodium. When you are on a low-carb diet, you will need more salt.

**LOW SODIUM SYMPTOM:**

\-weakness

\-muscle cramps

\-constipation

\-palpitation

\-headaches

\-fatigue
