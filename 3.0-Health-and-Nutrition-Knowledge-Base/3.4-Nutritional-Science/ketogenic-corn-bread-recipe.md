# Ketogenic Corn Bread Recipe

**Ingredients:**

2 cups almond flour

4 large eggs

1/4 cup butter

1/2 tbsp. baking powder

1/2 tsp. salt

1/2 cup sour cream

10-12 Drops Stevia

**Instructions:**

1\. Add almond flour, eggs, and butter

2\. Mix well

3\. Add baking powder and salt

4\. Add sour cream and stevia drops

5\. Mix

6\. Bake for 35 Minutes at 350 Degrees
