# What are the worst “keto-friendly” low-carb foods?

- Soy protein isolate

- Casein

- Maltitol

- Aspartame

- Processed cheese

- Common processed meats: lunch meat, hot dogs, bacon

- Rancid nuts

- Margarine

- Iceberg lettuce

- Fast food burger/chicken

- Pork rinds with MSG

- Vegetable oils

- Corn fiber

- Farm-raised fish

- Low-fat dairy
