## Natural Remedies for Glaucoma: Eye Health Support Glaucoma is a degenerative condition that affects the optic nerve in the eye, leading to progressive vision loss and potentially irreversible blindness if not properly managed. Discover six natural remedies for glaucoma that may help manage symptoms, prevent vision loss, and promote eye health. ## **What is glaucoma?** Glaucoma is a progressive eye disease that develops gradually as damage to the optic nerve accumulates over time. The optic nerve is responsible for transmitting visual information from the eye to the brain. As nerve damage worsens, it can lead to significant vision loss and, eventually, blindness. The most common type of glaucoma is primary open-angle glaucoma (POAG), which develops slowly and often has no noticeable early warning signs. This explains why glaucoma is generally referred to as the silent thief of sight. Other types include acute angle-closure glaucoma, normal-tension glaucoma, and secondary glaucoma.
